Data Preparation & EDA

Flight data was scraped from online paragliding platforms and stored as KML/IGC files. Weather records were retrieved from reanalysis datasets. After cleaning and preprocessing, GPS trajectories were merged with weather variables such as wind speed, temperature, and pressure.

  • Data cleaning: removing incomplete flights
  • Coordinate transformation & altitude correction
  • Feature engineering: climb rates, distances, time of day
  • Exploratory Data Analysis: distributions, correlations, visualization
