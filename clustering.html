Clustering & Analysis

To identify patterns in flying conditions, unsupervised learning methods were applied. Clustering helps group flights by similarity in features such as duration, maximum altitude, or climb rate. This allows us to discover typical “flight profiles” and associate them with specific weather conditions.

  • K-Means clustering on flight performance metrics
  • DBSCAN to detect unusual or extreme flights
  • Correlation of clusters with weather predictors
